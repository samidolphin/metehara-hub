Matahara Embraces Digital Transformation! New One-Stop Service Center Officially Opens 💻🚀

መንግሥት የሕዝብ አገልግሎቶችን በዘመናዊ ቴክኖሎጂ በማዘመን ፈጣን፣ ቀልጣፋና ጥራት ያለው አገልግሎት በመስጠት የህዝብ እርካታን ለማረጋገጥ በትኩረት እየሠራ መሆኑን የምሥራቅ ሸዋ ዞን አስተዳዳሪ አቶ አባቡ ዋቆ ገልጸዋል። በምረቃ ሥነ-ሥርዓቱ ላይ የመክፈቻ ንግግር ያደረጉት የመተሐራ ከተማ ከንቲባ አቶ አዲሱ ዋቆ፣ ይህ አዲሱ የአንድ ማዕከል አገልግሎት (One-Stop Service Center) መንግሥት ለዜጎች ማዕከል ያደረገ አገልግሎት የሚሰጥበት ቁርጠኝነት መገለጫ መሆኑን ጠቁመው፣ ማዕከሉ አላስፈላጊ ቢሮክራሲን በመቀነስ ለህብረተሰቡ ፈጣንና ቀልጣፋ አገልግሎት ለማድረስ ከፍተኛ ሚና እንደሚጫወት ገልጸዋል። 🤝✨ በዚሁ መሠረት፣ በምሥራቅ ሸዋ ዞን መተሐራ ከተማ የተገነባው የአንድ ማዕከል አገልግሎት (One-Stop Service Center) በይፋ ተመርቆ ለሕዝብ ክፍት ሆኗል። 🎊✅ የማዕከሉ ዋና ጥቅሞች፦ ⏱️ ፈጣንና ውጤታማ የሕዝብ አገልግሎት መስጠት። 🤝 ቢሮክራሲን በመቀነስ የዜጎችን እንግልት ማስወገድ። 🖥️ በዘመናዊ ቴክኖሎጂ የተደገፈ ግልጽ፣ ተጠያቂነት ያለው አገልግሎት መስጠት።
Mootummaan tajaajila hawaasaa teeknoloojii ammayyaatiin sirneessuudhaan quufinsa ummataa mirkaneessuuf xiyyeeffannaan hojjechaa jiraachuu Bulchaan Godina Shawaa Bahaa obbo Abbaabbuu Waaqoo ibsaniiru. Hojii misoomaa kana ilaalchisuudhaan haasawa baninsaa kan taasisan Kantiibaan Bulchiinsa Magaalaa Mataaharaa obbo Addisuu Waaqoo; wiirtuun kun mootummaan tajaajila nama giddu-galeessa godhateef xiyyeeffannaa kennuusaatiin kan agarsiisu ta'uu eeranii, hawaasni biirokraasii hin barbaachisne irraa bilisa ta'ee tajaajila saffisaa akka argatu gahee guddaa kan qabu ta'uu dubbataniiru. 🤝✨ Haaluma kanaan, Godina Shawaa Bahaa, Magaalaa Mataaharaatti "Wiirtuun Tajaajila Iddoo Tokkoo" (One-Stop Service Center) haaraa ijaarame eebbifamee ummataaf banaa ta'eera. 🎊✅ Faayidaa Wiirtichaa: ⏱️ Kenniinsa tajaajilaa daran saffisaa fi bu'a-qabeessa taasisuu. 🤝 Biirokraasii xiqqeessuudhaan gidiraa ummataa hambisuu. 🖥️ Teeknoloojii fayyadamuudhaan hojii iftoominaa fi itti gaafatamummaa qabu uumuu.
The government is working with strong commitment to modernize public service delivery through advanced technology in order to improve efficiency and ensure greater public satisfaction, East Shewa Zone Administrator Mr. Abbabu Wakoo stated. Speaking at the inauguration ceremony, Matahara City Mayor Mr. Addisu Wakoo emphasized that the newly established One-Stop Service Center reflects the government's commitment to providing citizen-centered public services. He noted that the center will play a significant role in eliminating unnecessary bureaucracy and enabling residents to receive faster, more efficient services. 🤝✨ Accordingly, the newly constructed One-Stop Service Center in Matahara City, East Shewa Zone, has been officially inaugurated and opened to the public. 🎊✅
